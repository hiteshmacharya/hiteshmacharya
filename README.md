## Research & Publications

### AI-Augmented B2B Sales Intelligence at Scale  
**Large Language Models · RAG · AWS Bedrock · LangChain · Agentic Workflows**

A production-oriented framework for reducing enterprise sales research cycles from **four weeks to approximately 30 minutes** using LLMs, RAG pipelines, vector search, and multi-agent orchestration.

- Covers AWS Bedrock, LangChain, OpenSearch, structured data ingestion, quality evaluation loops, and enterprise adoption models.
- Validated across **12,847 intelligence briefs** for **8 Fortune 500 organisations**.
- Demonstrates cycle-time reduction, improved output quality, and significant per-brief cost reduction.

[Read paper](https://jsetms.com/view_issue.php?title=AI-Augmented-B2B-Sales-Intelligence-at-Scale)

---

### Applying Hybrid Deep Learning to Behavioural Pattern Recognition in Enterprise Platforms  
**BERT · CNN · BiGRU · Enterprise SaaS Analytics · Behaviour Modelling**

A hybrid deep learning architecture combining **BERT, CNN, and BiGRU** for user behaviour modelling in SaaS environments.

- Designed for churn prediction, feature adoption forecasting, and anomalous behaviour detection.
- Introduces behavioural tokenisation for enterprise action logs.
- Reports improved F1-score performance over single-architecture and ensemble baselines.

[Read paper](https://jsetms.com/view_issue.php?title=Applying-Hybrid-Deep-Learning-to-Behavioural-Pattern-Recognition-in-Enterprise-Platforms)

### Predicting Energy Demand Using Machine Learning  
**Time Series · Weather Features · LSTM · Forecasting · Energy Analytics**

A machine learning study focused on short-term energy demand prediction using temporal patterns and weather-related variables.

- Evaluates Linear Regression, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and LSTM models.
- Highlights the importance of temperature, temporal encoding, and meteorological features in forecasting accuracy.
- Provides practical implications for grid operations, renewable integration, and demand planning.

[Read paper](https://ajaccm.com/journal/index.php/ajaccm/article/view/444/415)

---

### ML-Based Demand Forecasting: Lessons from Temporal and Weather-Driven Energy Models  
**XGBoost · LightGBM · LSTM · TCN · Temporal Fusion Transformer · Forecasting Systems**

A practical engineering framework for integrating meteorological variables, temporal decomposition, uncertainty quantification, and deep learning in energy demand prediction.

- Compares statistical, gradient-boosted, and deep learning forecasting models.
- Demonstrates the value of weather-augmented modelling and regime-aware ensembles.
- Includes production deployment lessons for monitoring, model selection, and operational reliability.
